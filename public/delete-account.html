Delete your DIGIRINGO account and data

Applies to the DIGIRINGO app (developer: DIGIRINGO) and to digiringo.com. Last updated: 23 July 2026.

You can ask us to delete your DIGIRINGO account and the personal data in it at any time, free of charge. You can also ask us to delete specific data — such as your message history or your voicemail recordings — without closing your account.

A. Delete your whole DIGIRINGO account

  1. Send an email to support@digiringo.com from the email address your DIGIRINGO account is registered with.
  2. Put Delete my account in the subject line.
  3. We reply within 2 business days to confirm it is really you. We may ask you to confirm from the registered address or to verify a phone number on the account.
  4. Once confirmed, we delete the account within 30 days and email you when it is done.
Email us to delete my account

B. Delete specific data — and keep your account

You do not have to close your account to have data removed. You can ask us to delete any of these on their own:

  • your SMS and MMS messages and conversation history;
  • your call history and call records;
  • your voicemail recordings and greetings;
  • your saved contacts and blocklist;
  • the business-verification (10DLC) details you submitted.
  1. Send an email to support@digiringo.com from your registered email address.
  2. Put Delete my data in the subject line.
  3. In the message, name what you want deleted — for example “delete all my message history” or “delete my voicemail recordings”.
  4. We confirm it is you within 2 business days, then delete that data within 30 days and email you when it is done.
Email us to delete specific data

Your account, your phone numbers and your wallet balance stay exactly as they are. Billing records are kept for the period shown below, because tax law requires it.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted we permanently remove:

  • your account and profile — name, email address, password hash, workspaces and settings;
  • all SMS and MMS messages and conversation history;
  • all call history and call records;
  • all voicemail recordings and greetings;
  • saved contacts, blocklists, forwarding numbers and number settings;
  • your push-notification token and device identifiers;
  • any business-verification (10DLC) details you submitted, except where a carrier registry is required to retain them.

What is kept, and for how long

DataKept forWhy
Billing and transaction records (amount, date, invoice) Up to 7 years Required by tax and accounting law. These records do not contain your messages or call content.
Carrier call and message logs (numbers, time, duration) As required by our telecom carrier and applicable telecom regulation Held by the carrier that transmitted the call or message, not usable by us to identify your account after deletion.
Anonymous, aggregated statistics Indefinitely Cannot be linked back to you or to any individual.
Security and abuse records where an investigation is open Until the matter is resolved Fraud and abuse prevention.

Everything else is deleted. Backups are rotated out within 90 days.

Before you delete — this cannot be undone.
  • Every phone number you rent is released. A released number goes back to the carrier pool, can be given to someone else, and cannot be recovered.
  • Any remaining wallet balance is forfeited and is not refundable.
  • Active plans and subscriptions are cancelled.
  • Your message and call history is gone — export or screenshot anything you need first.
If you only want to stop being charged, reply asking us to cancel your plan instead of deleting the account.
